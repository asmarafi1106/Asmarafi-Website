About Plaza Balikpapan

Plaza Balikpapan is the heart of urban lifestyle in Balikpapan, offering a dynamic blend of shopping, dining, and entertainment. Conveniently located in the city center, this iconic mall is a go-to destination for locals and tourists alike. From trendy fashion outlets and cutting-edge electronics to cozy cafés and gourmet restaurants, Plaza Balikpapan caters to every taste and need. Whether you're catching the latest blockbuster at the cinema, meeting friends for coffee, or simply enjoying a day of retail therapy, Plaza Balikpapan delivers a vibrant and modern experience that reflects the energy of the city.

Beyond shopping and entertainment, Plaza Balikpapan also serves as a community hub, hosting various events, exhibitions, and seasonal celebrations that bring people together. Its sleek, contemporary design and welcoming atmosphere make it a perfect spot for families, young professionals, and travelers looking to immerse themselves in the local scene. With its prime location and diverse offerings, Plaza Balikpapan stands as a symbol of the city’s growth and lively spirit.
